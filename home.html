Yona's sweet treats

I started selling sweets when I gave birth to my daughter last 2018, she was diagnosed with Neonatal Sepsis, she's having a hard time breathing on her own and needs an oxygen support in full blast while incubated.

She remained inside the NICU for more than 3 weeks and our hospital bill reached P280,000 pesos. That's the time I decided to sell leche flan, to fund our hospital bill. It boomed and I sold more than 300 pcs in just 2 days. and that's the start of converting it into a small business.
